Who are we?

SquareHelp is a tool designed for the smoker who needs extra motivation to quit smoking. SquareHelp will allow a user to have full-access to the member community of individuals who are on also on the same journey. SquareHelp can meet the smoker where they are at in their journey to quitting and remind them they are not in this alone.
